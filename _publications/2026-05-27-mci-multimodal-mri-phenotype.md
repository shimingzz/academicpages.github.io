This study analyzes baseline MCI participants from the Kaohsiung Veterans General Hospital cohort and compares three-year converters with stable MCI participants using multimodal MRI features.

Preliminary results indicate broad white matter abnormalities, including lower NODDI neurite density index across TractSeg white matter bundles, with convergent diffusion and fixel-based measures across limbic-prefrontal, commissural, cerebellar, fronto-pontine, thalamic, and striatal systems.
