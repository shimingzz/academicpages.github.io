This planned AI study uses ADNI and local morphometric similarity networks to evaluate representation learning, domain shift, calibration, and graph-model interpretability for near-term dementia conversion.

Current feasibility results include ADNI and local morphometric MSN datasets with matched graph dimensions and measurable cross-domain similarity, supporting a domain-aware graph-learning design with leakage-safe validation and direct clinical-baseline comparisons.
