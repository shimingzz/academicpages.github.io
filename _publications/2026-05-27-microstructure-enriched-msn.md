This study compares morphometric, DTI-enriched, and NODDI-enriched morphometric similarity networks in the same local MCI cohort.

Preliminary results suggest that the NODDI-enriched MSN produces the most stable C-MCI > S-MCI network-based statistic components, with dominant visual-limbic and visual-default organization. Exploratory prediction remains interpreted conservatively against clinical severity baselines.
