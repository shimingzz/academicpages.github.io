Designed and trained GCN, GAT, and GraphSAGE models to classify MCI-to-dementia conversion from MRI-derived brain graphs. The workflow pretrains on ADNI and fine-tunes on the ILAS cohort to address cross-site domain shift. Model interpretation uses GNNExplainer to connect predictions back to regions and graph structure.
